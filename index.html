Round1(Tecasino)

Player starts with 5000 virtual coins using those he can buy questions which are of 4 levels,If he is not sure about the answer he has got an option to sell the question (questions will not be revealed) level 1 - 50 coins level 2 - 100 coins level 3 - 200 coins level 4 - 500 coins Solve the given question to earn double the points.

Round2(solve and conquer)

Starts with the points they have earned at the end of round 1.They will buy countries using those coins for which the countries are priced accordingly.At the end of round, team having highest valued countries will be the winner.
